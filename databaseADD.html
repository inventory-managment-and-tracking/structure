📐 Database Design Plan

10 tables · Full QR flow · Staff logins · Stock history · Alerts · Reports · Returns

📌 Database engine recommendation: MySQL 8 or PostgreSQL 15 — both support all data types above. For a web app serving 1–5 staff simultaneously, either works perfectly.
🔍 Indexing tip: add indexes on products.sku, stock_movements.product_id, sales.created_at, returns.sale_id for fast QR lookup and report queries.
